Play Crowns and Anchors

         

To play, simply type the amounts of your bets in the boxes below the spaces you wish to bet on. You may bet on any number of spaces, in any amount up to your current total (actually you can bet more than that, but you should never gamble on credit). When your bets are placed, click "Roll the dice" and see where they land. If one or more land on squares you bet on, you win your original bet times the number of dice that rolled that square (plus your original bet, of course). If not, you lose your bet on that space. You start with 2 shillings (24 pence), about 3 day's wages for a skilled worker in 1580. Good luck!
